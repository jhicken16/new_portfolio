Speech To Text Commands

In response to the limitations I’ve encountered with previous speech-to-text applications, I’ve developed a solution to address these issues. The challenge lies in the manual correction process—deleting words or adding punctuation disrupts the flow. With my application, users can define custom commands and exert greater control over the resulting outcomes.
Development is ongoing for this site. There's an issue with detecting single spoken commands depending on how the browser speech recognition listen for spoken word. There is also a latency issue as the functionality acts as middleware for React-Speech-Recognition. This can be improved by searching for commands at a lower level within the two-dimensional probability array.
